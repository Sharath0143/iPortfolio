Everest

Project information

  • Technology: ASP.Net MVC, C#.Net, jQuery, Autofac, Dapper, SQL Server
  • Environment: Visual Studio, SQL Server
  • Client: The Himalaya Drug Company
  • Role: Developer

Everest Latvia is a web-based application that will enable the Customers and Customer Support team to access the portal & place orders online. Customers can be categorized as SNOP & Non-SNOP Customers, For SNOP customers the Orders will be derived by Primary Order Advice, then they can modify it if required and submit the orders, whereas for Non-SNOP customers they can place the orders directly in the portal and submit the orders. Each customer is mapped based on their Service Point and based on their Service Point the Sales orders will be automatically created. Based on the series of the product manufactured, the orders will be diverted to respective Manufacturing Hubs.
